Chapter 01

We Lost Money to a
Fake Concert Ticket.

It started with a Dua Lipa concert. We wanted to buy from a private seller but had no way to verify the ticket was real. We ended up overpaying on Ticketmaster. Later, when we tried reselling Ariana Grande tickets, the platform automatically listed them at a higher price but would only pay us the lower amount we originally set.

We started asking around. Our classmates at UT Austin told us the same stories: bots scooping football tickets in seconds, fake QR codes burning people on resale, hidden fees making a $50 show cost $80. Artists we talked to said they earn nothing when scalpers flip their tickets for 3x the price.

That's when we realized this isn't just our problem. It's everyone's problem.
